# Phase 2: Advanced Risk Quantification

## 1. Introduction

This phase converts phishing behavior into organizational and financial risk.

---

## 2. Organizational Risk Exposure

Credential Rate (Campaign B) = 75 / 250 = 30%

Expected Compromised Users  
= 2,000 × 0.30 = 600

Expected Breaches  
= 600 × 0.25 = 150

---

## 3. Financial Impact

Single Breach Cost = $4,350,000  

Total Exposure  
= 150 × $4,350,000  
= $652,500,000  

---

## 4. Annualized Loss Expectancy (ALE)

ALE = SLE × ARO  
= $4,350,000 × 150  
= $652,500,000  

---

## 5. Control Analysis

### MFA

Cost = $45 × 2,000 = $90,000  
Risk Reduction = $652,500,000 × 0.999 = $651,847,500  

ROI  
= ($651,847,500 − $90,000) / $90,000  
= 724,175%

---

### Training

Cost = $100,000  

New Rate = 30% × (1 − 0.60) = 12%  

New Exposure = 2,000 × 0.12 = 240  
New Breaches = 240 × 0.25 = 60  

New ALE = $261,000,000  

Risk Reduction = $391,500,000  

ROI = 391,400%

---

## 6. Summary Table

| Control | Cost | New ALE | Risk Reduction | ROI |
|--------|------|--------|---------------|-----|
