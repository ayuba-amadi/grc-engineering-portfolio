# Risk Quantification Methodology

## Overview

This project applies quantitative risk analysis to evaluate cybersecurity threats in financial terms.

---

## Key Concepts

### 1. Single Loss Expectancy (SLE)

Represents the financial loss from a single incident.

SLE = Asset Value × Exposure Factor

---

### 2. Annual Rate of Occurrence (ARO)

Represents the expected frequency of occurrence per year.

---

### 3. Annualized Loss Expectancy (ALE)

ALE = SLE × ARO

This is the primary metric used to prioritize risks.

---

## Control Evaluation Approach

Each control is evaluated using:

- Risk Reduction = ALE × Control Effectiveness  
- ROI = (Risk Reduction − Cost) / Cost  
- Payback Period = Cost / Risk Reduction  

---

## Decision Logic

Controls are selected based on:

- Highest financial impact  
- Highest ROI  
- Fastest payback  
- Coverage of critical risks  

---

## Outcome

This methodology ensures that cybersecurity decisions are:

- Data-driven  
- Financially justified  
- Aligned with business priorities
